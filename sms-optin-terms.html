SMS Opt-In Terms

By opting into Victory Vision Digital Out of Home (“Victory Vision”) SMS notifications, you consent to receive automated text messages relating to your digital billboard campaigns. Message frequency varies depending on your account activities.

Message Types

  • Campaign status updates
  • Analytics and performance notifications
  • Scheduling confirmations and reminders
  • Customer support messages

How to Opt-In

You consent to receive SMS notifications by:

  • Submitting consent through our online forms at vvdoh.com, or
  • Providing verbal consent during documented onboarding or customer support calls.

How to Opt-Out

You can stop receiving messages anytime by replying STOP to any message. After opting out, you will no longer receive SMS updates. For assistance, reply HELP.
